# Produce Intelligence

**Input:** verified findings, audience, decision requirement, and disclosure constraints.

**Output:** report or export containing findings, evidence, confidence, gaps, and integrity metadata.

1. Lead with the answer required by the investigation objective.
2. Cite the evidence behind every material claim.
3. Distinguish fact, inference, assessment, and unresolved question.
4. Include provenance, relevant hashes, scope, and limitations.

**Stop:** the product is reproducible from its cited evidence and discloses no unauthorized data.

**Script:** optional sibling `run.el`.
